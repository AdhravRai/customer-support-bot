GigaCorp Customer Support Knowledge Base

==================================================
Shipping Policy
==================================================

Q: Do you offer international shipping?
A: Yes. GigaCorp ships to more than 50 countries including India, the United States, Canada, Australia, Germany, and Singapore.

Q: How long does shipping take?
A: Domestic shipping usually takes 2–5 business days. International shipping usually takes 7–14 business days depending on customs clearance.

Q: How much does shipping cost?
A: Domestic shipping is free for orders above $50. Orders below $50 are charged a $5 shipping fee. International shipping charges vary depending on destination and package weight.

==================================================
Returns & Refunds
==================================================

Q: What is your return policy?
A: Customers may return products within 30 days of delivery if the items are unused and in their original packaging.

Q: How long do refunds take?
A: Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after the returned product has been inspected.

Q: Can I exchange a product?
A: Yes. Exchanges are available for defective or damaged products within 30 days of purchase.

==================================================
Business Hours
==================================================

Q: What are your customer support hours?
A: Customer support is available Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM UTC.

Q: Is customer support available on weekends?
A: Weekend support is available only through email. Live chat operates only on weekdays.

==================================================
Orders
==================================================

Q: Can I cancel my order?

A: Orders may be cancelled within 2 hours of purchase if they have not yet been shipped.

Q: Can I change my delivery address?

A: Delivery addresses may be updated before the order enters the shipping process.

==================================================
Warranty
==================================================

Q: Do products include a warranty?

A: Most electronic products include a one-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects.

==================================================
Payment
==================================================

Q: Which payment methods do you accept?

A: GigaCorp accepts Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, and major debit cards.
