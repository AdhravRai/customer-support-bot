GIGACORP CUSTOMER SUPPORT KNOWLEDGE BASE

SECTION 1: SHIPPING POLICIES AND RATES
--------------------------------------
[Domestic Shipping (United States)]
- Standard Shipping: Cost is $5.99, but is free for all orders over $50. Delivery timeframe is 3 to 5 business days.
- Express Shipping: Cost is $14.99. Delivery timeframe is 1 to 2 business days.
- Shipping Carriers: Domestic orders are shipped via FedEx, UPS, or USPS depending on the destination.

[International Shipping]
- GigaCorp ships to over 150 countries worldwide, including Canada, Europe, the United Kingdom, India, and Australia.
- Shipping to India:
  * Standard International Shipping: Cost is $29.99. Delivery takes 10 to 15 business days.
  * Express International Shipping: Cost is $49.99. Delivery takes 3 to 5 business days.
- Shipping to Canada:
  * Standard International Shipping: Cost is $9.99. Delivery takes 5 to 7 business days.
  * Express International Shipping: Cost is $24.99. Delivery takes 2 to 3 business days.
- Shipping to Europe & UK:
  * Standard International Shipping: Cost is $19.99. Delivery takes 7 to 10 business days.
  * Express International Shipping: Cost is $39.99. Delivery takes 3 to 4 business days.
- Constraints: We do not ship to P.O. boxes, APO/FPO military addresses, or remote territories for international shipments. Import duties and taxes are not pre-paid and are the responsibility of the customer.

SECTION 2: RETURN AND EXCHANGE PROCESS
--------------------------------------
[Return Policy]
- GigaCorp offers a 30-day return policy for most items.
- Items must be returned in their original packaging, unused, and with all original tags attached.
- Refund Method: Refund will be processed to the original payment method within 5 to 7 business days after the returned item is inspected at our warehouse.

[Return Shipping Costs]
- Standard Tier members: A flat return shipping fee of $4.99 will be deducted from your final refund amount.
- Gold and Platinum Tier members: Return shipping is 100% free. A prepaid shipping label will be provided.

[Exchange Process]
- Customers can exchange an item for a different size or color within 30 days of purchase.
- To initiate an exchange, visit the GigaCorp Online Return Portal, select the exchange option, and print the return label.
- The replacement item is packaged and shipped to the customer as soon as the carrier scans the return package.

[Non-Returnable Items]
- The following items are final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged:
  * Gift cards
  * Personalized or customized products
  * Clearance, warehouse sale, and final markdown items
  * Software or digital downloads once accessed or downloaded

SECTION 4: CUSTOMER SERVICE MEMBERSHIP TIERS
--------------------------------------------
[Standard Tier]
- Eligibility: Automatic upon registering a basic account on gigacorp.com.
- Benefits:
  * Access to standard shipping and return rates.
  * Customer support via email and phone (standard queues).
  * Basic order tracking.

[Gold Tier]
- Eligibility: Annual spend of $500 or more, or via a subscription of $9.99/month.
- Benefits:
  * Free Standard Shipping on all orders (no minimum purchase required).
  * Free Returns on all eligible items.
  * Priority Support: Dedicated phone queue and email responses guaranteed within 4 hours.
  * Early access to clearance events.

[Platinum Tier]
- Eligibility: Annual spend of $1500 or more, or via a subscription of $19.99/month.
- Benefits:
  * Free Express Shipping on all orders (no minimum purchase required).
  * Free Returns on all eligible items.
  * 24/7 dedicated VIP phone and chat line.
  * Dedicated Personal Shopping Assistant.
  * Extended 60-day return policy.
